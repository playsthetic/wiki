# Playsthetic

Welcome to our wiki. Here you will find in depth information about our conventions and workflows, as well as useful resources that we keep in mind while working on our projects.

## Contribute

You will find links on each page allowing you to edit them directly via GitHub. Alternatively, you can also do that by cloning the [repository](https://github.com/playsthetic/wiki) on your local machine.

::: tip
The wiki's role is to present objective facts about working at Playsthetic. It is not a place to store propositions and ideas. For this purpose, check out our [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/playsthetic/discussions).
:::
